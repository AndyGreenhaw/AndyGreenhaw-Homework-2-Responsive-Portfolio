About Andy

Andy Greenhaw is a digital marketing strategist, animator, video editor, writer, and former journalist. He’s worked on projects for the Air Force, Special Forces, the Defense Intelligence Agency, National Defense University, and some of the top attorneys across the county.

As the Digital Strategist for High Impact, Andy administered the creation and deployment of content through High Impact’s website, product pages, case studies, blog, bi-weekly email blast, trade show brochures, and social media. He also managed High Impact’s Google Adwords, Analytics, and general search engine optimization (SEO). Before High Impact, Andy worked as the Digital Strategist for a marketing agency called Hangar 30 that specialized in communications projects for U.S. military clients.

Andy has twelve years of experience in digital marketing, twelve years of experience in video editing, nine years of experience in computer animation, seven years of experience in email marketing, eight years of experience writing for Search Engine Optimization, five years managing Google Adwords.
